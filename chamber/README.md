# Suien Swimwear Site Plan (水苑)


## Site Name

**Suien (水苑)**  

The name *Suien* combines a modern and elegant sound with its Japanese meaning related to water and garden I love Japan and its cultute, and my family comes from an Iland thats why I decided to do a store related to the sea one of the things I love the most in life.


## Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an online ecommerce platform where users can:

- Browse and purchase swimwear for **men, women, and kids**
- Explore **beach accessories**
- View detailed product information such as **price, color, and description**
- Experience a **clean, modern, and user-friendly interface**


## Target Scenarios

### Scenario 1
A user wants to find a stylish swimsuit for an upcoming vacation.  
→ What options are available for women, and what are the prices?

### Scenario 2
A customer is looking for beach accessories like fins or sunglasses.  
→ What products are available and how can they easily view details?


## Color Schema
| Primary | #2CA9BC | Headers, buttons, highlights |
| Dark Accent | #1F3A44 | Text, navigation |
| Light Background | #F4F8F9 | Page background, cards |
| Secondary | #6C8A91 | Borders, subtle text |
| CTA | #F4A261 | Call-to-action buttons |
---

## Typography

- **Primary Font:** Lato  
  - Used for body text  
  - Clean and easy to read  

- **Secondary Font:** Montserrat  
  - Used for headings  
  - Modern and bold appearance  



## Wireframe Structure

### Mobile View
- Header with logo and hamburger menu  
- Hero section  
- Product cards in a **single column layout**  
- Footer with contact and social links  

### Desktop View
- Full navigation menu  
- Hero banner  
- Product grid (**3 columns**)  
- Footer organized in two columns  


## Development Approach

The website follows modern web development practices:

- Semantic HTML structure  
- External CSS styling  
- Responsive design using **Grid and Flexbox**  
- Clean and organized class naming
